Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2025

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Honeycomb ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Honeycomb is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the App's functionality.
  • Financial Data: The financial information you enter in the App is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud backup, this data is also stored in your personal iCloud account.
  • Authentication Data: If you enable biometric authentication, we use Apple's authentication services, but we never have access to your biometric data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Detect and prevent errors or issues

Data Storage and Security

All your financial data is stored locally on your iOS device. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. When you enable iCloud backup, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is subject to Apple's privacy practices and security measures.

Third-Party Services

Honeycomb uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud: For data backup and synchronization (when enabled by you)
  • Apple Authentication: For biometric authentication (when enabled by you)

Each of these services has their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

Children's Privacy

Honeycomb is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
